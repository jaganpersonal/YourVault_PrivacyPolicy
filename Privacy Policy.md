Privacy Policy – YourVault

Last updated: Feburary 2026

1. Introduction
YourVault (“the app”) is a privacy-first, offline Android application designed to store secrets and sensitive information locally on the user’s device.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.
2. Information We Collect
YourVault does NOT collect any personal data.
The app does not collect, store, or process:
Name
Email address
Phone number
Location
Contacts
Photos or media
Device identifiers
Usage analytics
3. Data Storage
All data entered into YourVault is stored only on the user’s device.
Data storage (Keystore).
No cloud storage, backup servers, or remote databases are used.
4. Internet Usage
YourVault works completely offline.
The app does not connect to the internet.
No data is transmitted outside the device.
5. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade user data.
Since no data leaves the device, third-party access is not possible.
6. Permissions
The app requests only the minimum permissions required for its functionality.
No sensitive or unnecessary permissions are used.
7. Security
User data remains fully under the user’s control.
Reasonable security practices are followed to protect locally stored data.
Users are responsible for securing their device (PIN, password, biometric, etc.).
8. Children’s Privacy
YourVault does not collect any personal data.
Because no data is collected, stored, or shared:
Children under the age of 13 are allowed to use this app.
The app complies with child-privacy requirements by design.
9. Third-Party Services
YourVault does not use:
Ads
Analytics tools
Tracking SDKs
Google Play services are used only for app distribution and license verification, not for data collection.
10. User Control
Users have complete control over their data.
All data can be removed at any time by:
Clearing app data, or
Uninstalling the app
11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected in this document.
12. Contact
For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the YourVault GitHub repository.
